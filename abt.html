About us

The concept of “Streetwear” as a lifestyle has been burgeoning since the early 2010’s and shows no emblem of declining since its inception. “Streetwear” which was once deemed as a ragged style of clothing has now been imbibed by not only the fast fashion brands but also all the major leading luxury fashion brands. Now, narrowing it down to sneakers, a “Streetwear” segment which was once dominated solely by “Sneaker-heads”, has now become prevalent amongst all millennials and Gen-Z with the advent of Hip-Hop culture. Although it becomes extremely difficult to get your hands-on these sneakers given their exorbitance, consumers have become more inclined towards “re-selling” of these sneakers, wherein existing owners put their shoe on sale for other consumers to bid on. Re-selling has become a norm in the sneaker industry and hence, this is something we would want to emphasize on through our e-commerce website.

We developed "Sneaker-X" a C2C e-commerce platform that bridges the gap between the users (sneaker owners) and the consumers (coveting for those sneakers) and hence, allows for “Re-selling” of the same. The two cardinal entities of our platform would be the sneaker owners and the consumers. The focus of the platform would be allowing users to put up their pair of sneakers (along with other accessories) for sale on our platform. The users will be rendered with the flexibility to provide a minimum and a maximum expected price out of those sneakers, which would then be displayed on the platform to our user base. Now, for every pair, the minimum price will be the starting point from which the bidding on those respective pairs of sneakers may begin
